Data Integration

According to Experian, 66% of companies lack a centralized approach to data, with data silos being one the of the most common issues. Data silo happens when fixed data remains under the control of one department and is isolated from the rest of the department due to different channels through which information is collected. This results in poor communication between departments, systems and processes that affect business decisions. At Sageo Consult, our IT specialists will consolidate your business data residing in different sources to provide you with a real-time unified view of business performance.
